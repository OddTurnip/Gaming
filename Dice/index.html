Dice Rollers

Basic Dice Roller

Roll basic polyhedrals (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, and d100).

Custom Dice Roller

Roll custom-sized polyhedrals, up to 1,000 dice at a time.

Fate Dice Roller

Roll Fate/Fudge dice (4dF). Each die shows plus (+), minus (-), or blank, with results ranging from -4 to +4.

Blades in the Dark Dice Roller

Rolls d6s for Blades In The Dark. Your high result determines your result: 1-3 is a failure, 4-5 is a mixed result, and a 6 is a success. For 0 dot actions, the lowest result of 2d6 is used instead.

About

This is a collection of online dice rollers for tabletop gaming. Each roller is designed for specific gaming systems and keeps a history of your rolls during your session.

More dice rollers will be added in the future to support additional gaming systems and mechanics.
